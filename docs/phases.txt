Project Phases Overview

- Modular project structure
- Separation of bodies, physics, solvers, integrators, engine

- Forward Euler (non-symplectic)

Phase 2 — Validation & Diagnostics (Complete)
- Energy, angular momentum, linear momentum
- Center-of-mass tracking
- Euler vs Leapfrog comparison

Phase 5 — Performance & Scaling (Complete)

- Direct vs Barnes–Hut crossover
